Purpose

The Appointment System is designed to help users manage their appointments efficiently. It allows users to schedule, view, and manage their tasks seamlessly.

How It Works

Users can fill out the appointment form with their details, including name, username, email, task list, subject name, and description. Once submitted, the information is processed and stored for future reference.

Features

  • User-friendly interface
  • Easy appointment scheduling
  • Task management capabilities
  • Email notifications (coming soon)
